# Investment Guidelines — Indian Real Estate

## Investment Decision Framework

### Buy Indicators
- Price-per-sqft is below micro-market average by 10-20%
- RERA-registered project with clear title
- Proximity to upcoming infrastructure (metro, highway, airport)
- Developer track record: 3+ completed projects with timely delivery
- Rental yield > 3% with appreciation potential > 8% YoY
- Inventory months < 18 in the micro-market

### Hold Indicators
- Property is at market average pricing
- Market is in a transitional phase
- Infrastructure benefits are 2-5 years away
- Current rental yield covers maintenance + loan interest

### Sell / Avoid Indicators
- Price-per-sqft exceeds micro-market average by 20%+
- Inventory months > 24 in the micro-market
- Developer has history of delays or legal issues
- No upcoming infrastructure catalysts
- Market sentiment is bearish with declining transaction volumes

## Risk-Return Profiles by Property Type

### Conservative Investment
- **Type**: 2BHK Apartment in Tier-1 city suburbs
- **Expected Return**: 7-10% annual (appreciation + rental)
- **Risk Level**: LOW
- **Horizon**: 3-5 years
- **Suitable For**: First-time investors, retirement planning

### Moderate Investment
- **Type**: 3BHK Apartment in Tier-1 city prime locations
- **Expected Return**: 10-15% annual
- **Risk Level**: MODERATE
- **Horizon**: 5-7 years
- **Suitable For**: Experienced investors, portfolio diversification

### Aggressive Investment
- **Type**: Pre-launch projects in emerging micro-markets
- **Expected Return**: 15-25% annual (if timed correctly)
- **Risk Level**: HIGH
- **Horizon**: 3-5 years
- **Suitable For**: Risk-tolerant investors with liquidity buffer

## Tax Implications

### Stamp Duty & Registration
- Varies by state: 4-8% of property value
- Additional 1% metro cess in some cities
- Women buyers get 1-2% concession in many states

### GST on Under-Construction Properties
- Affordable housing (< ₹45 Lakhs, < 60 sqm carpet): 1% GST (no ITC)
- Non-affordable housing: 5% GST (no ITC)
- Ready-to-move properties: No GST applicable

### Income Tax Benefits
- **Section 80C**: Principal repayment up to ₹1.5 Lakh/year
- **Section 24(b)**: Interest deduction up to ₹2 Lakh/year (self-occupied)
- **Section 80EEA**: Additional ₹1.5 Lakh for affordable housing (if eligible)

### Capital Gains on Sale
- **Short-term (< 2 years)**: Taxed at income slab rate
- **Long-term (≥ 2 years)**: 20% with indexation
- **Section 54**: Exempt if reinvested in residential property within 2 years
- **Section 54EC**: Exempt if invested in specified bonds within 6 months (up to ₹50 Lakhs)

## Due Diligence Checklist

### Legal Verification
- [ ] RERA registration verified on state portal
- [ ] Title deed verification (minimum 30 years of chain)
- [ ] Encumbrance certificate (EC) obtained
- [ ] Building approval and commencement certificate verified
- [ ] Occupancy certificate (OC) for ready properties
- [ ] Society formation status (for apartments)

### Financial Verification
- [ ] Price comparison with RERA-reported prices
- [ ] Carpet area vs super built-up area ratio calculated
- [ ] Total cost including stamp duty, registration, GST, maintenance deposit
- [ ] Home loan pre-approval obtained
- [ ] EMI-to-income ratio < 40%

### Location Verification
- [ ] Physical site visit conducted
- [ ] Neighborhood infrastructure inspected
- [ ] Distance to workplace, schools, hospitals verified
- [ ] Future development plans checked with local authority
- [ ] Flood zone / seismic zone assessment

## Financing Guidelines

### Home Loan Eligibility (General Rules)
- EMI should not exceed 40% of net monthly income
- Loan-to-Value (LTV): Up to 80% for loans > ₹75 Lakhs
- LTV: Up to 90% for loans ≤ ₹30 Lakhs
- Maximum tenure: 30 years (most banks cap at 65 years age at maturity)

### Interest Rate Types
- **Fixed Rate**: Higher initial rate, protection against rate hikes
- **Floating Rate**: Lower initial rate, varies with RBI repo rate
- **Hybrid**: Fixed for 2-5 years, then floating
- **Recommendation**: Floating rate preferred when rates are expected to decline

### Down Payment Planning
- Minimum: 10-20% of property value
- Recommended: 25-30% to reduce EMI burden
- Additional costs: 8-12% of property value (stamp duty, registration, GST, legal, interiors)
